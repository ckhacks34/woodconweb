About Us

Our Story

WOODCON is a premier supplier of African wood products, dedicated to sustainable forestry practices and ecological responsibility. Founded with a vision to showcase the beauty and durability of African woods while promoting environmental conservation.

We work directly with local communities across Zambia and other African countries to ensure fair trade practices and economic empowerment while preserving natural resources for future generations.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, sustainable wood products that celebrate African craftsmanship while protecting our forests and supporting local communities.
