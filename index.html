About Us

Our Story

Woodcon Limited is a Zambian timber company founded in 2010 by Mr. Luke Selesitino. The company specializes in timber processing, production, and construction.

Woodcon is committed to providing high-quality timber products to its customers.

Our Mission

Woodcon Limited is committed to providing high-quality timber products and services to both its local and international customers while maintaining sustainable forestry practices.

The company strives to always efficiently provide innovative solutions and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

Woodcon Limited aims to be the leading timber company in Zambia, providing high-quality timber products to customers both locally and internationally.

The company is committed to sustainable forestry practices and aims to be a model for responsible timber production.

Products and Services

Timber Processing

We transform premium hardwoods from sustainable forests into high-quality finished products. Our specialty includes mukwa, teak, mahogany, and other fine woods crafted into custom furniture, doors, and architectural elements.

Construction Services

The company offers construction services as well as timber processing services such as sawing: planks commercially/domestically.

Railway Supplies

We produce standard sawn cants or railway slippers and completed bulk supply of railway slippers to Zambia Railways (October, 2023).

Machinery Hire

We offer machinery/equipment hire services for timber-related projects.

Our Wood Materials

At WOODCON, we work with a variety of sustainably sourced timber species, each with unique properties and applications:

Mukwa

Premium hardwood from Bovu Local Forest, known for its durability, rich color, and resistance to termites. Ideal for high-end furniture and structural elements.

Teak

Durable and naturally termite-resistant wood from Malanda Forest. Teak's natural oils make it excellent for outdoor furniture, decking, and moisture-prone areas.

Mahogany

Rich reddish-brown wood perfect for fine furniture, carvings, and architectural details. Valued for its beautiful grain patterns and workability.

Additional Woods

We also work with mopane, rosewood, and pine (available on special order). Each wood type is carefully selected for specific project requirements and environmental considerations.

All our wood materials are sourced from responsibly managed forests. We maintain strict sustainability practices to ensure minimal environmental impact while delivering exceptional quality.
