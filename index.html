About Us

Our Story

Woodcon Limited is a Zambian timber company founded in 2010 by Mr. Luke Selesitino. The company specializes in timber processing, production, and construction.

Woodcon is committed to providing high-quality timber products to its customers.

Our Mission

Woodcon Limited is committed to providing high-quality timber products and services to both its local and international customers while maintaining sustainable forestry practices.

The company strives to always efficiently provide innovative solutions and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

Woodcon Limited aims to be the leading timber company in Zambia, providing high-quality timber products to customers both locally and internationally.

The company is committed to sustainable forestry practices and aims to be a model for responsible timber production.

Products and Services

Timber Processing

We process diverse timber species such as mukwa, teak, mahogany, mopane, and rosewood from Bovu Local Forest in Kazungula and Malanda Forest to make finished products such as desks, doors and a variety of other forms of timber products. Softwoods like pine are available upon special order.

Construction Services

The company offers construction services as well as timber processing services such as sawing: planks commercially/domestically.

Railway Supplies

We produce standard sawn cants or railway slippers and completed bulk supply of railway slippers to Zambia Railways (October, 2023).

Machinery Hire

We offer machinery/equipment hire services for timber-related projects.

Recently Completed Projects

  • Completed bulk supply of railway slippers to Zambia Railways (October, 2023)
  • Ministry of education (Kazungula) 60 desks supply (January, 2024)
  • Fitted 50 desk Bottoms and Tops at Maranatha Adventist (February, 2024)
  • Supplied Ucolm Enterprise with 50 desk tops and bottoms (February, 2024)
  • Supplied Chimilute Trust Academy with 11 Desk Tops and Bottoms (February, 2024)
